## Positioning (honest)
Qualtrics is an enterprise Experience-Management (XM) platform for rigorous research. Youform is a modern,
affordable survey/form builder. The page targets people who find Qualtrics **too expensive / overkill / hard**,
NOT enterprise researchers who need conjoint/FedRAMP. Be honest about where Qualtrics genuinely wins.

## Verified differentiators (Youform's favor)
- **Pricing transparency**: Qualtrics has NO public pricing, quote/contact-sales only, annual contracts. [REPORTED]
  Reportedly low-thousands to $100k+/yr; commonly cited ~$28k median. HEDGE all $ figures ("reportedly"). [UNCERTAIN]
  Youform: public, flat — Free $0, Pro $29/mo ($20/mo annual).
- **Unlimited responses**: Qualtrics meters responses (free = 500 total; paid = negotiated allotment + overage fees).
  Deleted AND partial responses still count against the license. [OFFICIAL/REPORTED] Youform = unlimited.
- **Free plan**: Qualtrics free "account" is a trial, not a real plan: **500 responses total, 3 active surveys,
  30 questions/survey, no email distribution, no integrations/API, deleted after 13mo inactivity**. [OFFICIAL]
  Youform free = unlimited forms/questions/responses + skip logic + export.
- **Modern conversational UX**: Qualtrics is traditional paged web forms (multi-question pages). One-question-at-a-time
  is NOT native — requires manual block/branch/page-break engineering. [REPORTED, incl. Typeform's comparison]
  Youform = native conversational, one question at a time with animation → higher completion, esp. mobile.
- **Ease of use**: Qualtrics = powerful but steep learning curve, "non-intuitive, requires training", consultant-heavy
  setup (weeks/months). G2 ~4.4/5 but usability is the top complaint. [REPORTED]

## CORRECTION — partial submissions (user assumed Qualtrics may not have it; IT DOES, robustly)
- Qualtrics auto-saves responses-in-progress on **page navigation**; configurable timeout (1h…1yr); "Save & Continue"
  resume; partials recorded to "Responses in Progress". [OFFICIAL]
- KEY GOTCHA: saving only happens on page navigation, so a **single-page / conversational survey saves nothing**
  until submit — Qualtrics docs tell you to add page breaks. [OFFICIAL]
- DO NOT claim Youform has partials and Qualtrics doesn't. Honest angle: Youform captures partials natively on
  modern one-page/conversational forms (where Qualtrics' paged model needs workarounds), and it's not gated behind
  enterprise cost/complexity. Frame as "simpler / works out of the box," not "they lack it."

## Where Qualtrics genuinely wins (honest "choose Qualtrics if…" section)
Conjoint/MaxDiff/TURF statistical analysis; full XM suite + Text iQ; academic/market-research rigor; managed
respondent panels; enterprise compliance: **HITRUST, FedRAMP High, DoD IL4, ISO 27001/27017/27018/27701**. [OFFICIAL]
Youform does not target these and shouldn't pretend to.
